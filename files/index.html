Its Rockstar Dashing Harika's Birthday — Memory Page

A small prank, a long drive, and lots of ice cream. Photos & video from the celebration.

About the Night

We celebrated Harika's birthday with our students — laughter, songs, and an unforgettable ice-cream party. We even pranked Karthik and his team by saying we were headed to Mysore for a palace night view; instead we enjoyed a fun drive to HSR Layout in the 2200 chariot.

We clicked photos, enjoyed an elevated flyover drive, had ice cream till 1:30 AM, and then safely dropped everyone back at ALC. Big thanks to Harika and to all the students who made the night special.

Highlights: Ice-cream jars, candid laughs, group photos, and warm memories.
